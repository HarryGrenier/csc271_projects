About Us

A Little About Us

Growing up, I found it difficult to find information regarding fishing the Rhode Island coast. Any good information I received was from family and friends over the years.
This site hopefully simplifies that task for anyone new to the area or those who have grown up here and are interested in learning a little more.

Problems We Plan to Solve

Through this site, you can expect updated regulations and local fishing spots in the area with information regarding:

  • What to expect to catch here
  • Where it's located
  • Species native to the area
  • Recommended equipment
  • Regulations

What Makes Us Special

This site aims to simplify everything you might need to get started fishing in the area.
Here, we have all the information you could need, from regulations to recommended equipment.
