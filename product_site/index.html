Fishing around Rhode Island

About us

A little about us

Growing up I found it difficult to find information regarding fishing the Rhode Island coast. Any good information I received was from family and friends over the years.
This site hopefully simplify that task for anyone new to the area or grown up here who is interested in learning a little more.

Problems we plan to solve

Through this site you can expect updated Regulations allowing ease of access. Along with this Local fishing spots in the area with information regarding to:

  • What to expect to catch here
  • Where its located
  • species native to the area
  • recommended equipment
  • Regulations

What makes us so special

This site is aimed to simplify everything you might need to get started fishing in the area.
here we have all the information you could need from Regulations to recommended equipment
