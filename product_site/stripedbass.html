Striped Bass are one of the most popular fish around the new england coast. So popular that some people consider them a trophy fish.
They are distinguished by there unique black and white stripes down there sides along with the large bass mouth. they are often found
along side Bluefish in more generally rocky areas.

What they eat:

  • Bait fish
    • Bunker
    • Mackerel
    • herring
  • Squid
  • Eels
  • Clams
  • Crabs

Where can they be found:

Striped bass can be found in a verity of areas such as docks, Rocky structure (break walls), or reefs.
one major thing to keep in mind is they like to hide in/near structure.

Some popular spots they are found around here:

  • Fort Adams, Newport RI
  • Beavertail lighthouse, Jamestown RI
  • Point Judith Lighthouse, Narragansett RI
  • Block Island
    • North rip
    • South east side of the island
    • West side of the island
  • Charlestown Breachway, Charlestown RI
