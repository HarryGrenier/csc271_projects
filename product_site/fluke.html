Summer Flounder (Fluke)

Summer Flounder (Fluke) are often targeted by anglers for their ease of catching along with
their great taste. Common bycatches are other bottom fish along with striped bass.

What They Eat:

  • Squid
  • Bluefish Strips
  • Dogfish Strips

Where They Can Be Found:

Summer Flounder (Fluke) like sandy bottoms and can be found in deep or shallow water. They can be found along beaches, sandbars, or even piers.

Some Popular Spots They Are Found Around Here:

  • Harbor of Refuge, Narragansett RI
  • Point Judith Lighthouse, Narragansett RI
  • Jim's Dock, Narragansett RI
  • South side of the island, Block Island RI
